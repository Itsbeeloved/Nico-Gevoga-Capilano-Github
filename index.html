About me

I'm a passionate designer with a focus on graphic design. I believe in creating thoughtful, visually compelling work that resonates with audiences and solves real-world problems. My approach is driven by a mix of creativity, research, and continuous learning. I'm always excited to explore new design challenges and collaborate with others to bring ideas to life. I notice the little details in everything and I try to let that carry through to my work.

In my work, I strive to blend minimalism with innovative technologies to create impactful experiences. When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring local art galleries, experimenting with cooking, or hiking through nature trails. My journey into design began during college when I discovered how visual communication could transform ideas into impactful solutions. I chose this field because I believe thoughtful design has the power to both delight and solve meaningful problems.
